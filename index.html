X-men: Dark Phoenix

Plot

The X-Men face their most powerful enemy when one of their own, Jean Grey, starts to spiral out of control. Her mom is acciendently killed in a car crash and Jean is sent away to a school of young gifted people. Nine years later, during a rescue mission in outer space, Jean is nearly killed when she's hit by a mysterious cosmic force. This force not only makes her more powerful, but far more unstable. The X-Men must now join together to save her soul and battle aliens that want to use Grey's new abilities to rule the galaxy.
